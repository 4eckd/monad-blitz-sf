**What is thirdweb?**

thirdweb is the developer platform for building the next generation of internet products. The platform provides everything from wallet infrastructure, token creation, bridging, blockhain specific AI, and tools for native internet payments. thirdweb removes the complexity of interacting with the blockchain so you can focus solely on building.
