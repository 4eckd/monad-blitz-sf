# Submission Process

### Steps to prepare your project repo:

1. Visit the `monad-blitz-sf` repo (link [here](https://github.com/monad-developers/monad-blitz-sf)) and fork it.

![image.png](attachment:c52e268c-11e4-40dc-821c-4ab49d371cb6:image.png)

1. Give it your project name, a one-liner description, make sure you are forking `main` branch and click `Create Fork`

![image.png](attachment:127e386c-dc06-4a6c-a3ae-621b563c065c:image.png)

1. In your fork you can make all the changes you want, add code of your project, create branches, add information to `README.md` , you can change anything and everything.
2. Submit your repo and project info at https://blitz.devnads.com/
