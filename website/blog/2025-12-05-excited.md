# Ready for Monad Blitz SF #18

Tomorrow, December 6th, marks something special for me. I'll be participating in Monad Blitz SF #18, and honestly, I'm a mix of excited and nervous about pulling this off in just 11 hours.

## The Challenge

## Technology as a Path Forward

I'm a justice-impacted person. That's not something I often lead with, but it's part of my story. Technology has always been my creative outlet, my way of finding my spot in life when traditional paths were closed to me.

While I was inside, I couldn't code. But I could think, plan, dream. I studied algorithms in my head, designed systems on paper, and promised myself that when I got out, I'd build something meaningful.

MACHUPS is that promise kept.

## Redefining What's Possible

Tomorrow, I'm not just building an app. I'm challenging what it means to be justice-impacted in tech.

Too often, people with criminal records are written off. We're told our mistakes define us, that we can't be trusted with complex work, that we should be grateful for whatever opportunities we get.

I reject that narrative.

Justice-impacted individuals aren't just capable of working in techâ€”we can **innovate** in tech. We can build production-ready systems. We can compete in hackathons. We can contribute meaningfully to the ecosystem.

We just need the chance.

## Why This Matters

Events like Monad Blitz SF #18 are crucial because they judge ideas, not backgrounds. They evaluate what you build, not where you've been.

That's the world I want to help create:
- Where a formerly incarcerated developer can build an AI platform
- Where your commit history matters more than your criminal history
- Where innovation is open to everyone

## The Plan

Tomorrow at 11:30 AM PST, I start. By 10:30 PM PST, I need to have:
- A working brand generation platform
- Live documentation at docs.machups.com
- Smart contracts deployed on Monad Mainnet
- NFT certificates minting successfully
- A 3-minute demo ready

11 hours to go from zero to production.

I've planned it down to the hour:
- **H0-3:** Foundation (AI integration, logo generation, design tokens)
- **H3-7:** Core features (components, documentation, exports)
- **H7-9:** Blockchain (smart contract, NFT minting)
- **H9-11:** Polish, deploy, demo

## My Hope

If you're reading this and you're justice-impacted, formerly incarcerated, or facing barriers to entry in techâ€”know that you belong here.

Your past doesn't define your potential. Your creativity, your problem-solving, your unique perspectiveâ€”these are assets, not liabilities.

Technology has always been a positive force in my life. It gave me purpose when I felt purposeless. It gave me a future when I couldn't see one. It gave me a community when I felt alone.

And now, maybe it can be that for someone else too.

## Tomorrow

Tomorrow, I'll be nervous. My hands might shake when I start coding. I'll probably hit bugs that make me question everything. There will be moments when I think I can't finish in time.

But I'll keep building. Because that's what we do.

We take our second chances and we build something meaningful.
