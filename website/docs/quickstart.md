# Quick Start

Get your first brand generated in under 5 minutes.

## Prerequisites

- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Monad wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, or Coinbase Wallet)
- Basic understanding of Web3 (for NFT features)

## Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

For premium features and NFT certificates, you'll need a Monad-compatible wallet.

1. Visit [machups.com](https://machups.com)
2. Click "Connect Wallet"
3. Choose your preferred wallet (MetaMask recommended)
4. Approve the connection request
5. The app will automatically switch you to Monad network

## Step 2: Enter Your Brand Details

Fill out the brand generation form:

- **Business Idea**: Describe your business in 1-2 sentences
- **Target Audience**: Who are your customers?
- **Style Preference**: Choose from Modern, Classic, Bold, or Minimal

Example:
```
Business Idea: Sustainable coffee delivery service for busy professionals
Target Audience: Urban millennials aged 25-40
Style: Modern
```

## Step 3: Generate Your Brand

Click "Generate Brand" and watch the magic happen:

1. Claude AI analyzes your input (~10s)
2. Brand strategy generated (~20s)
3. Logos created (~30s)
4. Design tokens exported (~15s)
5. Components generated (~45s)
6. Guidelines PDF created (~60s)

**Total time: <3 minutes**

## Step 4: Review Your Brand Package

You'll receive:

- 3 logo variants (PNG & SVG)
- W3C DTCG design tokens
- 30+ React components
- 20-page brand guidelines PDF
- Live preview deployment

## Step 5: Choose Your Tier

### Free Tier (No Payment)
- 1-hour preview
- View only
- No exports

### Starter - $10
- 24-hour preview
- Download all files
- NFT on Monad Testnet

### Pro - $49
- Permanent deployment
- Custom domain
- NFT on Monad Mainnet
- Genesis Edition badge

## Step 6: Download & Deploy

After payment:

1. Click "Download Package"
2. Extract the ZIP file
3. Find your assets in organized folders
4. Deploy to your project or use the preview URL

## Troubleshooting

**Wallet won't connect?**
- Ensure MetaMask is installed
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear browser cache

**Generation taking too long?**
- Check your internet connection
- Try again during off-peak hours
- Contact support if issue persists

**NFT not appearing?**
- Wait 30-60 seconds for blockchain confirmation
- Check transaction on Monad Explorer
- Verify correct wallet address was used

Need help? [Open an issue](https://github.com/4eckd/monad-blitz-sf/issues)
